Instructions
  • Click Create to create a process.
  • Click Advance clock to advance the time.
  • To move a process from Ready Pool to the CPU, click on the process that you want to move.
  • Click CPU to IO Pool to move the process currently running in the CPU to the IO pool.
  • Click IO to Ready Pool to move the process with completed IO from IO pool to Ready pool.
  • Click Preempt to preempt the process currently running in the CPU and move it to Ready pool.
  • Click Terminate to terminate the process currently running in the CPU.
